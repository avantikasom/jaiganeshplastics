About us

Shri Ganesh Plastics was established in 1982 by Mr. Satish Chauhan in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh. Jai Ganesh Plastics is the sister company of Shri Ganesh Plastics.We have more than 35 years of experience in plastic products manufacture. We manufacture all kind of medicine bottles, carrom powder bottles, sanitizer bottles, caps and other plastic sports goods like hulla hoop, skipping rope, shuttle cock, carrom coins etc. In national market we are known for our quality products, customer satisfaction and on time delivery because we understand our customers requirements and value their time. We are committed to delivering high-end quality services and continually strive towards improving and providing our valued customers a pleasing experience with us.

We provide B2B solution to the business customers, by providing best quality sports goods at very reasonable price.
